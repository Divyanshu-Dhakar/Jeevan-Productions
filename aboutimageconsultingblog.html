HOW AN IMAGE CONSULTANT CAN HELP YOU?


  • Do you recognize room for improvement in your appearance, communication, or behavior?
  • Have you decided to upgrade your image, but are wondering how to proceed?
  • Are you a business owner who is wondering if your employees living up to your expectations with regard to their image?

An image consultant can be your guide to improving how you look, act, and communicate. Whether you're an individual or a business, they help you shine in three key areas: your appearance, behaviour, and communication skills.

They offer various services like wardrobe consultations, colour advice, personal shopping, and even etiquette coaching. Image consultants work with a wide range of people, from politicians to professionals in different fields, and they can specialize in working with specific groups like men, women, or teens. When hiring an image consultant, it's important to find the right fit for your needs. Consider interviewing several consultants and assessing their expertise and certification levels. Certified consultants have demonstrated their commitment to professionalism and ethical standards.

To make the most of your investment, determine your budget and what you hope to achieve through image consulting. Fees can vary depending on the consultant's experience and the scope of your project. Once you've found a consultant you're interested in, interview them to discuss your goals and expectations. Checking references is crucial to ensure the consultant's track record aligns with your needs. AICI membership and certification assure a consultant's qualifications and dedication to delivering quality service.
